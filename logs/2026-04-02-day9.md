# Day9 Log - Embedded Competition Preparation & Project Direction Lock

## Summary
Today’s work was mainly focused on the preparation phase for the embedded competition rather than technical implementation.  
I did not continue hardware debugging today. Instead, I concentrated on clarifying the competition direction, narrowing the project topic, preparing team recruitment materials, and reaching out for potential guidance support.

This was an important project organization day.  
The core outcome of today was not “more code written”, but “a clearer competition path and project positioning”.

## Work Done

### 1. Competition Direction Confirmation
I continued evaluating the 2026 embedded competition ST track and compared the topic options with my current project foundation.

After analysis, I further confirmed that the most suitable direction for me is:

- **Topic 4: Industry 4.0 / Motor Control / Robotics**

This direction aligns well with my current project path:
- STM32
- mobile robot chassis
- motor drive
- closed-loop control
- AGV / robotics related system integration

I also clarified that the near-term objective of my `ros2-mobile-robot-system` project should be aligned with the embedded competition deliverable.

### 2. Project Positioning Adjustment
Today I made an important strategic decision:

- `ros2-mobile-robot-system` will not be treated as a separate long-term exploratory project for now
- instead, its current phase goal will be shifted toward the **embedded competition result**

That means the project will now prioritize:
- STM32-based chassis control
- motor drive and control logic
- embedded system implementation
- competition-oriented engineering delivery

ROS2-related expansion will remain valuable, but it will serve the competition track rather than expanding without limit at this stage.

### 3. Registration Information Preparation
I organized and refined the key registration information for the competition, including:

- team name
- project title
- technical platform
- topic direction
- project description
- target MCU selection

The current candidate version was narrowed to:

- **Team Name:** OdomFlow
- **Project Title:** 基于STM32G4的AGV底盘闭环控制系统
- **Track:** STMicroelectronics Track
- **Platform:** STM32 General-Purpose MCU / MPU Platform
- **Topic:** Industry 4.0 / Motor Control / Robotics

This gave the project a much clearer competition-facing identity.

### 4. Teammate Recruitment Preparation
Today I also prepared teammate recruitment materials for the class group.

This included:
- clarifying what kind of teammate I actually need
- defining expectations for cooperation
- writing a recruitment document explaining:
  - current project basis
  - intended competition direction
  - expected division of labor
  - what a teammate could gain from joining

The goal was not to casually “find someone”, but to recruit a partner who can genuinely contribute to the project.

### 5. Initial Teammate Screening
After posting the recruitment message, I started receiving responses.

Today’s focus was not to immediately finalize a teammate, but to begin the early screening process:
- who is genuinely interested
- who might be able to contribute
- who is only casually asking
- who may be suitable for longer-term cooperation

This is still an ongoing process.

### 6. Advisor Consultation Preparation
I also identified a potential faculty advisor and prepared a formal message for consultation.

The message introduced:
- my ROS2 navigation project experience
- my current STM32 + chassis direction
- my intention to participate in the embedded competition
- my hope to receive advice or guidance

This was an important step because the advisor is not only needed for registration, but may also influence later project support and legitimacy.

## Key Progress
Today’s progress was mainly about **project organization and competition preparation**, including:

- competition topic direction became much clearer
- project phase objective was aligned with competition delivery
- registration information was substantially refined
- teammate recruitment officially started
- faculty consultation preparation was completed

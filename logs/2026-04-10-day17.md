- Used the multimeter to measure battery voltage
- Confirmed that a single 18650 cell can read about 4.2V when fully charged
- Confirmed that the 2-cell battery box outputs about 8.4V, which means the cells are connected in series

### 2. Buck Converter Inspection
- Checked the buck converter module
- Confirmed that the module uses solder-through holes for `IN+`, `IN-`, `OUT+`, and `OUT-`
- Realized that proper wiring will require soldering instead of temporary loose insertion

### 3. Hardware Familiarization
- Reviewed the current components and their roles:

- Clarified the current hardware direction before formal wiring and soldering

## Current Understanding

- Buck converter will be used for voltage step-down
- TB6612 will use the external motor power path
- STM32 can temporarily remain on USB power during the early integration stage
- Proper common ground and reliable wiring will be important in the next stage
